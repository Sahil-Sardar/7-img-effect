Megumi Fushiguro

Megumi is a dark-haired teen with a stoic and detached personality. He is the son of an outcast from the Zen'in clan. He became a jujutsu sorcerer in exchange for the school supporting him and his sister following the separation of his parents. He was born with the Zen'in Family's esteemed Inherited Technique, the Ten Shadows Technique, whichallows him to generate a shadowy, liquid substance he can summon up to ten different Shikigami from, aswell store objects in it.

In the anime series, he is voiced by Yuma Uchida in the Japanese version and by Robbie Daymond in the English version. Daymond's other heroic roles include Mumen Rider in One Punch Man, Mitsuki in Boruto: Naruto Next Generations, Goro Akechi in Persona 5, Reinhard van Astrea in Re:Zero Starting Life in Another World, Gilthunder in The Seven Deadly Sins, Joe Kido and Armadillomon in Digimon, Jin Mo-Ri in The God of High School, and Spider-Man in the 2017 show of the same name.

Yuji Itadori

Yuji is an easygoing high school student with a kind disposition. After the death of his grandfather, he vows to help others so that he may die "surrounded by loved ones", and makes it a point to ensure the people around him attain what he considers a "proper death".

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara Kugisaki is a fairly short young woman, standing at 160 centimeters (5'3") tall. Her dyed orange hair, that is originally dark, matches the color of her orange eyes. Like every member of Jujutsu High, Nobara wears a custom uniform, although it didn't deviate too far from the basic design. Her uniform consists of brown tights, light brown shoes, a dark-blue button-down jacket, and a dark-blue pleated skirt.
